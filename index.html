The Declaration of Faith (Shahada)

أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا ٱللَّٰهُ وَأَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا رَسُولُ ٱللَّٰهِ

"I bear witness that there is no deity worthy of worship except Allah, and I bear witness that Muhammad is the Messenger of Allah."

This is the declaration that makes one a Muslim - the acknowledgment that Allah is the One True God, and Muhammad (peace be upon him) is His final messenger.

The Five Pillars of Islam

1. Shahada (Faith)

Declaration of faith in Allah and His messenger

2. Salah (Prayer)

Five daily prayers facing Makkah

3. Zakah (Charity)

Giving to those in need

4. Sawm (Fasting)

Fasting during the month of Ramadan

5. Hajj (Pilgrimage)

Pilgrimage to Makkah once in a lifetime

About Islam

What is Islam?

Islam is the complete way of life revealed by Allah (God) to humanity through His final prophet, Muhammad (peace be upon him). The word "Islam" means "submission to the will of Allah" and brings peace to those who follow it.

Core Beliefs

  • Belief in Allah: The One and Only God, Creator of everything
  • Belief in Angels: Allah's creations who worship and serve Him
  • Belief in Holy Books: The scriptures revealed by Allah (Torah, Gospel, Quran)
  • Belief in Prophets: From Adam to Muhammad (peace be upon them all)
  • Belief in the Day of Judgment: When all will be held accountable
  • Belief in Divine Decree: Allah's knowledge and control over all things

The Purpose of Life

According to Islam, the purpose of human life is to worship Allah alone, live righteously according to His guidance, and prepare for the eternal life in the Hereafter. Allah says in the Quran:

وَمَا خَلَقْتُ الْجِنَّ وَالْإِنسَ إِلَّا لِيَعْبُدُونِ

"And I did not create the jinn and mankind except to worship Me." (Quran 51:56)

Morality in Islam

Islamic morality is based on the teachings of the Quran and the example (Sunnah) of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). Key moral principles include:

  • Honesty and truthfulness in all dealings
  • Justice and fairness to all people
  • Compassion and mercy to all of creation
  • Humility and modesty in behavior
  • Patience and perseverance in hardship
  • Gratitude to Allah and kindness to parents
  • Respect for others' rights and dignity
  • Avoiding harm to oneself and others
